Our Story

Born from the Spirit of Giving

NayePankh Foundation was born during the Covid-19 pandemic with a simple mission — help those who have no one else to turn to. What started as a small initiative grew into one of India's largest student-led NGOs.

"NayePankh" means "new wings" — symbolizing our belief that every person, no matter their circumstance, deserves a chance to soar.

Milestones

2020

Programs

What We're Working On

Our programs address hunger, education, hygiene, and community development

Hunger Relief Program

Weekly meals for homeless individuals, the elderly, and stray animals. Volunteers cook at home and distribute at identified spots.

82% of monthly target reached

Education for All

Free books, notebooks and mentorship for underprivileged students. Virtual and in-person tutoring sessions by our interns.

67% of monthly target reached

Menstrual Hygiene Awareness

Camp drives and free sanitary pad distribution across rural and semi-urban areas, reducing stigma and promoting dignity.

75% of monthly target reached

Seasonal Clothes Drive

Collecting wearable clothes from donors and distributing to those in need, especially ahead of winter and monsoon.

55% of monthly target reached

Internship & Skill Program

Offering 30,000+ young Indians the chance to contribute, build skills, and earn certificates while serving the community.

90% of monthly target reached
